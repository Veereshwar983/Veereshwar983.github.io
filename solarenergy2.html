Solar Power for Your Home: Basic Concepts Solar power can help you achieve considerable savings if you implement it at your home, and it is the easiest renewable energy source to scale down for residential applications. There are two main types of solar power systems that are commonly used in residential buildings: Photovoltaic systems Solar water heaters The main difference between them is that the first provides electricity and the second provides heat, but in both cases you are saving money by using sunlight instead of drawing power from the electric grid. This article will give you general information about the price and performance of photovoltaic systems.
